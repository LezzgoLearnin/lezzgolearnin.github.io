About Us

Founded during the 2021 pandemic, Lezzgo Learning is a youth-powered nonprofit offering free academic help and college guidance for all students. It’s grassroots. It’s powerful. It’s for students, by students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build bridges between students and their future by offering free tutoring, mentorship, and real-world tools. Our vision? Equal opportunity learning—everywhere.
