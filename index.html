About Us

Lezzgo Learnin' is a youth-led nonprofit organization dedicated to helping students succeed by providing completely free tutoring and college advice. We're run by high school and college students who know what it's like and want to give back.

Our Mission

At Lezzgo Learnin', our mission is to make academic support and college readiness resources accessible to all students, regardless of background or income. We aim to build a future where every learner has the confidence, tools, and guidance to chase their dreams fearlessly.
